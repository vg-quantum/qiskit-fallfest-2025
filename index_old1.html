About the Fest

Qiskit Fall Fest is a globally coordinated series of events celebrating the open-source Qiskit ecosystem. Our local chapter brings together quantum-curious newcomers and experienced practitioners to learn, build, and connect. Expect hands‑on labs, lightning talks, and opportunities to collaborate on mini‑projects.
