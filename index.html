About the Fest

This year’s Vanguard Qiskit Fall Fest is a landmark celebration that coincides with 100 years of Quantum Science and marks 2025 as the Year of Quantum. It is more than just a technical conference — it is a showcase of how Vanguard crew are preparing for the next century of innovation. The event is designed to inspire, educate, and connect employees who are passionate about the transformative power of quantum computing, and it is exclusively open to Vanguard crew.

On October 16, our Main Day begins with a keynote highlighting the Vanguard–IBM collaboration on a groundbreaking Portfolio Optimization solution for the FIG group. This keynote sets the stage for a series of hands‑on, introductory workshops led by the VG Quantum Working Group. Sessions include Quantum & Qiskit 101 for beginners, guided notebook explorations for Beginner and Intermediate coders, and more advanced exercises for those eager to push their skills further. All workshops are offered in a hybrid format, accessible both in person and online, ensuring that every interested crew member can participate. A detailed agenda and in‑person logistics will be shared as the date approaches.

The day culminates in the launch of a week‑long challenge where participants can apply what they’ve learned to practical problems. Teams and individuals will have until October 24 to submit their solutions, and winners will be announced in November. To recognize the effort and engagement of our community, all challenge participants will receive a recognition certificate from IBM Quantum. Workshop participants will receive certificates of participation, while winners will earn special certificates and Qiskit‑branded goody bags. In addition, there will be fun opportunities to win exclusive Qiskit goodies by engaging in live quizzes and interactive sessions throughout the event.
