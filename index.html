About the Fest

This year's Vanguard Qiskit Fall Fest is a landmark celebration that coincides with 100 years of Quantum Science and marks 2025 as the Year of Quantum. It is more than just a technical conference â€” it is a showcase of how Vanguard crew are preparing for the next century of innovation. The event is designed to inspire, educate, and connect employees who are passionate about the transformative power of quantum computing, and it is exclusively open to Vanguard crew.

On October 16, our Kick Off Day begins with a keynote highlighting the Vanguar-IBM collaboration on a groundbreaking Quantum Portfolio Optimization solution for the FIG group. This keynote sets the stage for a series of hands on, introductory workshops led by the VG Quantum Working Group over next 1 week. Sessions include Quantum & Qiskit 101 for beginners, guided notebook explorations for Beginner and Intermediate coders, and more advanced exercises for those eager to push their skills further. All workshops are offered in a hybrid format, accessible both in person and online, ensuring that every interested crew member can participate. A detailed agenda and logistics will be shared in the teams channel.

The week culminates in the launch of a week long challenge where participants can apply what they've learned to practical problems. Teams and individuals will have until November 5 to submit their solutions, and winners will be announced nn 15th November. To recognize the effort and engagement of our community, all challenge participants will receive a recognition certificate from IBM Quantum. Workshop participants will receive certificates of participation, while winners will earn special winner certificates and Qiskit branded goody bags. In addition, there will be fun opportunities to win exclusive Qiskit goodies by engaging in live quizzes during interactive notebook sessions throughout the event.

Prepare for the Day

To help participants get the most out of the Fall Fest, we encourage some light prep work before the event. You can explore curated resources hosted on our internal GitHub space, which includes reading material and starter notebooks. In addition, reviewing the QFF 2024 materials and code samples will provide helpful context and familiarity with Qiskit fundamentals. This preparation is optional but highly recommended, as it will allow you to dive deeper into the workshops, engage more effectively during live sessions, and tackle the challenge problems with greater confidence.
