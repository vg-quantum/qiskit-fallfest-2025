About the Fest

This year’s Vanguard Qiskit Fall Fest celebrates 100 years of Quantum Science and marks 2025 as the Year of Quantum. The event is exclusively open to Vanguard crew.

On October 16, the main day features a keynote on the Vanguard–IBM collaboration for a Portfolio Optimization solution built for the FIG group. Introductory workshops cover Quantum & Qiskit 101, Beginner Coding Notebooks, Intermediate Coding Notebooks, and Advanced Coding Notebooks, conducted by the VG Quantum Working Group. Workshops can be attended in person or online, with detailed in‑person agenda and location shared closer to the date.

The day wraps up with challenges participants can work on until October 24. Winners will be announced in November. Certificates and goodies include: recognition certificates from IBM Quantum for all challenge participants, participation certificates for workshop attendees, Qiskit branded goodies for quizzes and workshops, and special goody bags for winners.
